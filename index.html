Introduction

I am a data scientist at IBM in New York where I build timeseries models for predictive maintenance across different industries.
I also do research on how we can domain-adapt LLMs for condition-based maintenance tasks.
I have an MS in Data Science from Columbia University and a BS in Computer Science from the University of Cyprus.

Some topics that I am interested in:

  • Few-shot learning for Timeseries Classification. Motivation: Predictive Maintenance where there are very few sensor data examples from engine failures available
  • Timeseries Anomaly Detection. Motivation: Plenty of healthy engine sensor data and rare events of engine failures (anomalies)
  • LLM Domain adaptation. Motivation: Domain-adapt LLMs for condition-based maintenance tasks
  • LLM Knowledge Distillation. Motivation: Distill knowledge from larger models into smaller ones that have smaller inference costs
